    Some of the Courses required in ARTS are listed below:

    BSC cs:

    BSc Computer Science, or BSc CS is a UG program that covers theoretical as well as practical knowledge about computer science, computer applications, and its applications. BSc computer science course is majorly aimed at developing qualified professionals and research fellows who can use computer technology to operate in any industry in the world.

    BCA:

    BCA course full form is Bachelor of Computer Applications, it is an undergraduate-level degree course in computer applications. The course mainly focuses on the development of computer applications and software. BCA graduates have a wide range of career opportunities available to them in the field of computer science, such as software development, database management, web development, etc.

    BSC CHEMISTRY:

    BSc Chemistry also known as Bachelor of Science in Chemistry, is a three-year undergraduate programme that covers Inorganic and Organic as core subjects from any recognised board. BSc Chemistry syllabus and subjects include Inorganic Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry, and Analytical Chemistry, as well as electives such as Analytical methods in Chemistry, Polymer Chemistry, and Industrial Chemicals and the Environment.

    BSC PHYSICS:

    BSc Physics is a fundamental course for students who have an interest in Physic subjects. The course prepares students for higher education or lands them on a decent job in a private or government organization. Check the scope of the BSc Physics course here.

    BSC MATHEMATICS:

    B.Sc Mathematics is a 3-year duration undergraduate course that deals with the discipline of mathematics along with the analysis of numbers. The focus is also on mathematical structure, transition, and space. Students have career options in the field of analytics, software development, and banks after BSc Mathematics.
